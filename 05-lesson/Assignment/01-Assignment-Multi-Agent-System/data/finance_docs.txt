=== FINANCE DEPARTMENT DOCUMENTATION ===

[REIMBURSEMENT FILING POLICY]
To file a reimbursement claim at Presidio:
1. Log into the Expense Portal at expense.presidio.com.
2. Create a new expense report and select the matching categories (e.g. Travel, Business Meals, Home Office Setup).
3. Upload itemized receipts for all expenses exceeding $25. Credit card statements are not accepted as itemized receipts.
4. Submit the report. Claims are reviewed by the Finance Audit team. Approved reimbursements are paid out via direct deposit.

[BUDGET REPORTS]
All monthly and quarterly budget reports are stored securely in the Finance Shared Drive.
Path: Shared Drives > Finance-Archive > FY2026 > Budget-Reports
Access requires a Finance department profile or manager authorization. If you require temp access, submit a request via access.presidio.com.

[PAYROLL PROCESSING SCHEDULE]
Presidio payroll processing schedule details:
- Employees are paid semi-monthly (twice a month) on the 15th and the last business day of the month.
- If the pay date falls on a weekend or bank holiday, payments are processed on the preceding business day.
- Direct deposits typically post to bank accounts by 9:00 AM on the official pay date.
- Submit timesheet approvals at least 3 business days before the scheduled pay date.
